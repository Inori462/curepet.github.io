Adopt a Cat

Characteristics

  • Independent and curious nature
  • Affectionate but on their own terms
  • Great for smaller living spaces
  • Low maintenance and clean animals
  • Good companions and stress relievers

Rescue Guidelines

  • Visit a vet for a thorough check-up
  • Ensure the cat is spayed or neutered
  • Introduce them slowly to their new home
  • Offer hiding spots to help them feel secure
  • Be patient and gentle during adaptation

Grooming Guidelines

  • Brush regularly to reduce shedding and hairballs
  • Keep litter box clean and fresh
  • Trim nails every couple of weeks
  • Clean ears and eyes gently
  • Provide scratching posts to maintain claw health
