Adopt a Rabbit

Characteristics

  • Quiet, gentle, and affectionate pets
  • Can be litter-trained and respond to their name
  • Love to explore and hop around freely
  • Form strong bonds with their owners
  • Best kept indoors or in safe, enclosed areas

Care Guidelines

  • Provide a spacious enclosure with room to hop
  • Ensure daily exercise and playtime
  • Feed a diet of hay, fresh veggies, and rabbit pellets
  • Keep their living area clean and well-ventilated
  • Schedule regular vet visits and vaccinations

Grooming & Maintenance

  • Brush regularly to prevent fur matting
  • Trim nails every few weeks
  • Check ears and teeth for health issues
  • Provide chew toys to keep teeth healthy
  • Ensure they stay cool in warm weather

Fun Facts About Rabbits

  • Rabbits have nearly 360-degree vision
  • They “binky” (jump and twist) when they're happy
  • Can live 8–12 years with proper care
  • Rabbits are very clean animals and groom themselves often
